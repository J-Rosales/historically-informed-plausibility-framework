# WORLD_CONSTRAINTS_MATTER_OF_BRITAIN

## Purpose
This document instantiates baseline world constraints for **The Matter of Britain**.

## I. World Identification
World / Setting Name: The Matter of Britain
Narrative Scope: Legendary Britain (late Roman to early medieval)
Version / Date: Example

## III. Baseline Constraint Domains

### Physical Reach
- Terrestrial and maritime only.

### Energetic Capacity
- Biological, mechanical, localized supernatural.

### Ontological Layers
- Causally interactive metaphysical layer (miracle, enchantment).

### Technological / Infrastructural Ceiling
- Fragmentary late Roman / early medieval.

### Epistemic Access
- Oral, clerical, elite mediated.

### Agency and Embodiment
- Embodied humans; limited supernatural agents.

## IV. Explicit Exclusions
- Interplanetary travel
- Industrialized magic

## VI. Stability Declaration
Stable and fixed.
